Company History

From a Closet to a Commercial Lab.

What started as a hobby in a spare bedroom quickly evolved into a full-scale commercial operation. Here is how MycoCraft grew from a small spore library into a nationwide supplier.

2020

The First Spore Print

It began with a single spore print of Blue Oyster mushrooms. Our founders, frustrated by the high contamination rates of online suppliers, decided to build their own DIY laminar flow hood and start isolating genetics on agar plates.

2022

The Local Market

After perfecting our liquid culture recipes, we began supplying local farmers markets and gourmet chefs with fresh Lion's Mane and Shiitake. The demand outpaced our small 10x10 grow tent within months.

2024

Scaling Up Production

We acquired our first commercial warehouse. We installed a massive industrial autoclave capable of sterilizing 500 grain bags per run, and built a proper ISO-7 cleanroom to ensure zero-contamination inoculations.

2026

Nationwide Launch

Today, MycoCraft ships premium all-in-one grow kits, isolated liquid cultures, and lab equipment to thousands of hobbyists and commercial growers across the country, maintaining our 99.9% sterility guarantee.

Our Standards

What Drives Us.

Uncompromising Sterility

Every inoculation is performed under Class 100 laminar flow. If a batch doesn't pass our rigorous quality control, it never ships.

Sustainable Sourcing

We source organic, locally grown rye berries and utilize biodegradable packaging whenever possible to minimize our ecological footprint.

Open Education

We don't gatekeep knowledge. Our blog and support channels are fully open to help anyone learn the intricate science of mycology.

Community First

We view our customers as fellow researchers. We constantly listen to feedback to refine our substrate recipes and genetic offerings.

Behind the Scenes

Inside the Cleanroom.

Our laboratory is the heart of MycoCraft. Built to ISO-7 standards, the air in our inoculation suite is completely exchanged and scrubbed through medical-grade HEPA filters every 60 seconds.

This extreme control over the environment is why our liquid cultures boast zero contamination and why our grain bags arrive ready for explosive mycelial growth. We treat our workspace like a surgical suite, because in mycology, sterility is everything.
