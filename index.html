The Science Behind
Massive Yields.

Contamination is the enemy of cultivation. We operate in ISO-7 cleanrooms in front of strict 99.99% HEPA filter flowhoods, ensuring every grain bag and agar plate is completely sterile before it reaches your door.

Pressure Sterilization

All substrates are hydrated to field capacity and pressure cooked at 15 PSI for a minimum of 2.5 hours to destroy endospores.

Isolated Genetics

We don't sell random spores. Our liquid cultures are carefully isolated on agar to ensure aggressive, fast-fruiting rhizomorphic growth.

Filter Patch Technology

Bags utilize a 0.2 micron filter patch, allowing necessary gas exchange for the mycelium while completely blocking out mold spores and bacteria.
