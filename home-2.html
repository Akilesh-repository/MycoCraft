Frequently Asked Questions

How long do grow kits last?

Un-inoculated sterilized grain bags and substrates can last up to 3-4 months if stored in a cool, dark, and dry place. Once inoculated, the timeline depends on the strain's colonization speed.

What is a Liquid Culture?

A liquid culture (LC) is living mycelium suspended in a sterile, nutritious liquid broth. Unlike spores, which need time to germinate and mate, LC begins colonizing the grain immediately upon injection, cutting weeks off your grow time.

Do you guarantee against contamination?

Yes! We guarantee our bags and cultures arrive 100% sterile and viable. If you notice contamination before you inject your bags, contact us within 14 days for a free replacement.
